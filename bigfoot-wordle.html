How to Play

Guess the secret 5-letter wilderness word in 6 tries. After each guess, the tiles reveal how close you are.

🦶 Hint button — reveals a thematic clue about the word any time you need it.

🗺️ Trail Map — shows all possible words in the game. Words you've already found are marked green.

All words are themed around Bigfoot, camping, and Leavenworth, WA. Common English words are also accepted as guesses.
